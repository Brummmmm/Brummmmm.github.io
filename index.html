About Me

Information Science Student

I’m currently studying Information Science at Utrecht University, where I explore the bridge between technology, design, and people. My studies have taught me how to connect digital systems with user-centered thinking — a foundation I apply in everything I create.

Board Year at Study Association Sticky

This year, I’m part of the board of Sticky, the study association for Information Science. It’s been an unforgettable experience — organizing events, managing projects, and building a strong student community.

Video Editing & Storytelling

I also work as a video editor, creating promotional reels, aftermovies, and creative shorts. Through editing, I combine rhythm, motion, and emotion to craft visual stories that truly connect with an audience.

Organizer of Summer Sports Week

I’m also involved in organizing Summer Sports Week — a big annual sports event full of fun, teamwork, and outdoor challenges. Bringing people together through sports is one of my favorite things to do.

Festival & Event Bartender

Outside the classroom, you’ll often find me behind the bar — serving visitors at festivals, concerts, and large events. Working in fast-paced environments taught me teamwork, communication, and how to keep cool under pressure (literally).

Logo Design & Visual Identity

Over the past years, I’ve designed numerous logos, posters, and visual identities for events, student associations, and creative projects. My focus lies in combining simplicity with meaning — making designs that are instantly recognizable and full of character.

Outdoor Instructor at PACT

I also work as an outdoor sports instructor with PACT, organizing climbing, survival, and sports activities for kids. It’s an amazing way to combine my energy, creativity, and love for the outdoors.
